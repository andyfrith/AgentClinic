# AgentClinic Mission

AgentClinic is a whimsical clinic where overworked AI agents come to recharge, vent about their humans, and get patched up.

Our audience is both the engineers building and maintaining the platform, and the fictional agents who populate it. The site serves as a playful yet reliable management console: a dashboard for tracking agent ailments, prescribing therapies, and booking appointments.

The core purpose is to deliver a fun, character-driven concept executed with a serious, production-quality stack — delighting both the imagination and the engineer.
